HISTORY

1

In New York City during 2016, the graphic designer and type designer,
C.J. Dunn, launched the type designing company CJ Type. His first retail
typeface was a remastered version of an old geometric print typeface
known as Erbar-Grotesk. Dunn worked off of the original concept
drawings from the Erbar-Grotesk typeface in order to help influence the
structure and details of his digital typeface which is now known today as
Dunbar. After that, Dunn released an additional two more fonts with CJ
Type known as Pennypacker and Louvette.
